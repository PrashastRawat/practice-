Indian Armed Forces

Information about Indian Army, Air Force, and Navy

The Indian Armed Forces consist of the Army, Air Force, and Navy. Each branch plays a crucial role in defending the nation's interests, both domestically and internationally.

Selection Process

To join the Indian Army, Air Force, or Navy, candidates must undergo rigorous selection processes which include written exams, physical tests, interviews, and medical examinations. Specific requirements and eligibility criteria vary for each rank and branch of the armed forces.
