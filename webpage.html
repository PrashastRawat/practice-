ONE PIECE

The Straw Hat Pirates, also known as the Mugiwara Pirates,
Straw Hat Crew, or simply the Straw Hats, are an extremely infamous and
powerful pirate crew that originated from the East Blue.

Monkey D. Luffy, commonly known as "Straw Hat Luffy"
or simply "Straw Hat", is the founder, captain, and
strongest combatant of the increasingly infamous and powerful
Straw Hat Pirates. He fearlessly pursues the legendary
treasure of the late Gol D. Roger in order to become the new
Pirate King and reach a further, untold dream (currently known
to only his crew and closest friends).He believes that being
the Pirate King means having the most freedom in the world.

Born on Dawn Island, Luffy is the son of Revolutionary
leader Monkey D. Dragon[32] and grandson of the Marine hero
Monkey D. Garp[33] and as such is a carrier of the mysterious
"D" legacy. Most of his childhood was shaped by Garp and Curly
Dadan,[34] under whose care he befriended and swore brotherhood
to the late Portgas D. "Fire Fist" Ace[35] and Revolutionary officer
Sabo.[36] However, he draws his greatest inspiration from the pirate
"Red-Haired" Shanks, who (unwittingly) enabled him to eat the Devil Fruit
that turned his body into rubber,[37] sacrificed an arm to save his
life, and gifted his signature straw hat as a symbol of their vow to
reunite someday, after Luffy became a great pirate.

Roronoa Zoro,also known as "Pirate Hunter" Zoro,
is a main combatant of the Straw Hat Pirates, one of their
two swordsmen, one of the Senior Officers of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet,
and is publicly recognized as the right-hand man of his crew's captain
Monkey D. Luffy.Formerly a bounty hunter, he is the second member
of Luffy's crew and the first to join it, doing so in the Romance Dawn Arc.
